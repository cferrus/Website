Make It

Our Mission

 

Our goal is to bring together students with schools and community partners to reflect on the fundamental skills needed to develop ones creative confidence as a maker.

We aim to let Makerspaces engage students to learn in and out of school, by providing opportunities to develop practical skills that increase student engagement and prepare children for the 21st-century job market.

Our expectation is clear, Learn by Trial and Error to explore your interests and develop creative solutions to real-world problems.

Our Services

 

Annual Maker Festival

We designed, planned out the Maker Festival annually then we organized to run the event with an innovative theme of the year.

Mentor - Innovator Match up

We connect those who would like to get some hands-on experiences with their skillset with the one who wishes to find a mentor for their projects or the maker space itself.

Customized curriculum and/or projects based on the needs of the institutions

It all begins with an idea. Maybe you want to launch a business. Maybe you want to turn a hobby into something more. Or maybe you have a creative project to share with the world. Whatever it is, the way you tell your story online can make all the difference.
